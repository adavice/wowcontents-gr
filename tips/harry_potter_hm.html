Harry Potter Hogward's Mystery


Become a Powerful Wizard
Your character in this adventure will evolve along specific lines that will change according to your answers at key moments. So it’s worth taking a moment to ponder on which answer best suits the kind of wizard you want to be!


Often while you’re playing, you’ll come across a teacher or other students who will pose questions to your character. Depending in your specific answer, the statistics of your character will have certain attributes raised; available attributes are “Courage”, “Empathy”, and “Knowledge”, which are symbolized respectively with a green shield, a pink heart, and a purple book. Questions and answers play a pivotal role in this adventure! You can either choose to answer broadly so that makes your character evolves in a balanced way, or otherwise aim to be very consistent in your answers.


In which case you may find that your character gets extremely strong along certain lines at the expense of other attributes, to the point that certain conversational options may cease to be available during character interactions.
