Angry birds 2


Angry birds game was named one of the greatest android games when it was introduced. It has since then developed and gained massive popularity through various games platform.
Angry Birds 2 is here with a new feature of a randomized property that doesn’t make two playthroughs of a level to be the same. But it still maintains using a big slingshot to fling birds into a delicate building occupied by pigs.
The time has come for a sequel! Angry Birds 2 is here, and our favorite birds appear to be angrier!


1. Get acquainted with your birds
Stages in Angry birds 2 are more or less randomized, and the best tool to get through these stages is to know the right bird to use at a particular point in time.
The ability of each bird is identified below for a quick reminder:
* The black bomb bird can cause a huge bite out of structures, especially the ones that are hard to move slightly.
* The bluebirds are used in taking out ice
* Due to the massive nature of Terence, it can plow through buildings provided you make a good use of the terrain features.
* The shout of the red birds is employed in destroying strong structures.
* Matilda drops an egg which is useful for hitting TNT crates present between 2 structures.
* Chuck [yellow one] has the ability of through wood as a result of its speed.
* The new bird has a looping manometer which could be of significant advantage when employed well. It also has the ability to take a stone out.


2. Learn how to make proper use of your environment
Angry birds 2 employ the use of several environmental arrangements. Making proper use of these environmental settings from the early stage could be a boost to how progressive you can be. Take for instance, take note of the flowers, they chew and spit anything that crosses their parts; this feature can be employed to bring down structures.

3. Rain the Golden Ducks
Angry bird has a new feature which allows you to rain your golden ducks on the pigs. The game comes with new feature such as five spells which you can unlock as you play through the stages. Getting used to this spell to know when to employ them is a wonderful trick to scale through. Aside from the raining golden duck features, you can decide to turn the blocks into ice or transform all the pigs into exploding balloons. One of the most significant features of the spell is that it gives you the ability to throw sardines into the stage to lure the Mighty Eagle who get attracted to it and helps you to clear the screen. You might want to save your spells for when you will really need them.


4. Avoid restarting
Restarting angry birds 2 could cost you a precious life that you might not find easy losing. We know the restart button has helped a lot in the previous angry bird's games, but it is not valid with Angry birds 2.


5. An important angry birds 2 cheats
If you eventually run out of life while playing and you can’t wait for one. You should consider trying out this trick by following these steps: Close the game Go to settings on your android device Unclick the automated date and time Set your clocks 3 hours ahead. This will provide you with an extra five lives anytime you log into the game. Ensure you set your time correctly after you have been awarded the new lives.


6. Boss Fights
Angry Birds 2 now entails new bosses! You have to source for environmental opportunities you can employ to bring the bosses down.

Watching numerous angry birds 2 ads is a useful trick if you want to be a successful payer. Watching enough videos will provide you with the tricks that will keep you going even if you run out of birds. It will also offer you arena entries and multiply your daily rewards.
